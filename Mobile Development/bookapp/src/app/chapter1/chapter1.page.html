Chapter1 | The start

Max is our rescue cat. We rescued him and he has rescued us from depression. He was a shy cat at the start, hiding under the bed until he trusted us. He even managed to escape outside and we had to tempt him with tuna to come back.

That was the start of his love of tuna. Over the years that has expanded to other foods including pork, chicken and shrimp.
