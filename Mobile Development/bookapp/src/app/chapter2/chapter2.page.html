Chapter2 | Hunting

Max is a hunter. Mice and birds mostly. He even brought home a grouse one summer to stash in my closet. He also has terminated the squirrel population in the area. He's a thoughtful cat however, and leaves half a squirrel for his humans. Sometimes on the porch and other times at the foot of the bed.

He spent one summer bringing live catches back and "trading" them for shrimp. He's very smart.
